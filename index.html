About me

I studied sociology and political science, which means I've always been more interested in why people do things than in the systems they build to do them. I've worked across advertising, logistics, and pricing — as a revenue manager, analyst, and product manager — before specialising in data product management. These days I work as a Senior Data Product Manager at Kleinanzeigen.de in Berlin, where I help teams turn messy data problems into useful products.

Alongside my day job, I take on data product consulting and AI enablement engagements — helping teams adopt AI tools and workflows, build data fluency, and get more value from the data they already have. I also run workshops on product strategy and practical AI for non-technical teams. I write about what I'm learning in practice: the frameworks, the failures, and what actually changes when you take AI seriously as a working tool.

Outside of work: hiking, reading (novels, psychology, philosophy), and spending time with my family and two-year-old daughter in Berlin.
