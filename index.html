About me

I studied sociology and political science, which means I've always been more interested in why people do things than in the systems they build to do them. These days I work as a Senior Data Product Manager at Kleinanzeigen.de in Berlin, where I help teams turn messy data problems into useful products.

I'm curious about what it means to work, create, and live well in a world being reshaped by AI. I've been using Claude Code as both a work and personal tool, and I write about what I'm learning — the frameworks, the failures, and the surprising things that happen when you treat an AI like a real colleague.

Outside of work: hiking, reading (novels, psychology, philosophy), and spending time with my family and two-year-old daughter in Berlin.
